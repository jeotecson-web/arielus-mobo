Platform Components

Four distinct modules to empower your complete booking ecosystem. ArielusMOBO connects all stakeholders in the booking ecosystem. Agents issue tickets, operators manage schedules, users book seamlessly, and admins oversee everything — all in one integrated platform.

Ticketing Agent

Empower agents with fast, reliable ticketing tools. Issue tickets, manage reservations, and serve customers efficiently — even without internet access.

Operator App

Operators can track schedules, monitor bookings, and manage fleet operations in real time. Designed specifically for mobility and ease of use.

User App

Give your customers the power to book tickets anytime, anywhere. A user-friendly interface ensures a hassle-free experience, both online and offline.

Admin Portal

Centralized control for administrators. Manage agents, operators, and users with powerful analytics, reporting, and configuration tools.
